About Us

We are the fans of Borussia Dortmund Club, united from all over the world. We publish fan favorite things in this site. The aim of this website is to bring all the fans of BVB FC, not just from Germany, but from all the different countries who are very passionate about the football played by our club.

The Best Fan Club In The World

First off, there is our Signal Iduna Park. Who would not feel proud being able to call that imperious venue 'home'? With the largest standing terrace in world football – just imagine one huge, welcoming living room with like-minded people keen on exchanging hugs and a sing-song, and there you have the Sudtribüne – and consistently the highest average attendance of any ground in the world, at over 80,000 per shot, it's hard not to fall in love with BVB.
